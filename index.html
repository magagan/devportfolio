About Me

I’m an experienced Quality Assurance professional, specializing in Test Automation for web app and android native app. I passed the certification of ISTQB-BCS Certified Tester Foundation Level. I can do automated testing and can also perform manual testing. I’ve work in different IT industry, started at consulting company where he acquired his skills and knowledge in Software Testing, next is from local base provider of Point of Sale system mainly providing solution for fast food and resorts businesses in the Philippines. I learned Automated Testing using PHP and Selenium when I was working in US Autoparts. Passionate to any Automated Testing using open-source project available. I also worked in organization that mainly focuses in providing IT solution to Air Cargo business as QA Engineer. I’m currently working in OLX Philippines as Test Automation Engineer mainly responsible in leading the test automation effort for Web app and Mobile app. He also focuses helping manual QA's to create, execute, and maintain automated scripts of Web and Mobile app.
